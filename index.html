OUR purpuse

The purpose of the MSA will be to serve the best interest of Islam and Muslims of Texas A&M University. Overall, the MSA shall:

  • Promote unity and joint action among the Muslims.
  • Conduct religious, social, cultural, and other activities in the best traditions of Islam.
  • Arrange and hold congregational prayers and Islamic festivals at appropriate times.
  • Present Islam to Muslims and non-Muslims and promote good relations and understanding between them.
  • Seek appropriate means to make the Islamic teachings known to all interested people.

    • What we do

    Community Service

    The Prophet, peace be upon him, said: “The most beloved people to Allah are those who are most beneficial to people. The most beloved deed to Allah is to make a Muslim happy, or to remove one of his troubles, or to forgive his debt, or to feed his hunger.” As Muslims we encourage ourselves as well as our brothers and sisters to serve the community by means of caring to the needy, providing food, and ultimately bringing love and joy into the world. At TAMUMSA we hold multiple, regularly occurring community service events in the heart of Bryan/College Station. We have also partnered with other universities across Texas to serve our community on the grandest scale possible!

    Fundraising

    The Prophet, upon be peace, said:“The believer’s shade on the Day of Resurrection will be his charity.” (Al-Tirmidhi). In effort to follow the prophetic tradition, every semester, TAMU MSA partakes in an initiative to help raise funds for charity. In the fall we partner up with Islamic Relief and Charity Week to compete in the Global Charity Week competition. And in the spring, we join other MSAs across Texas to compete in LSC’s Project Sadaqah. Alhamdulillah TAMU MSA has consistently raised the most money out of any school in Texas over the past few years!

    Social

    One of the best parts of being the only Muslim student organization on campus is our social events that bring together Muslims students from all over campus! Our social events are geared towards meeting new people and establishing long lasting friendships that follow you even after college. Our social officers plan regular socials to incorporate activities such as ice skating, potlucks, pumpkin patches, axe throwing, and more!!

    Outreach

    One of our primary objectives as the sole Muslim organization at Texas A&M is to represent and teach islam to our peers on campus. Through weekly da’wah tables as well as semesterly seminars, we as an MSA attempt to spread the guidance of Islam to our beloved TAMU community with the hope that Allah will soften their hearts to the truth of Islam. We also schedule regular da’wah training with experienced Da’ees and Shuyukh to ensure our members have all the proper tools needed to explain and spread Islam to their peers.

Prayer areas on campus

Below are areas of campus that offer places for all faiths to utilize.

Evans Library 5th Floor

A prayer and meditation room is available for all faiths to use. Ask the front desk of the library or refer to the library map for the location of the room. It is located at the far right corner when turning right at the elevators and facing the entrance of the floor. Qiblah is directed using the green arrow seen on the ceiling.

Location

All Faiths Chapel

This chapel is open for any religion to use. The building is right across the Beutul Health Center on campus. When entering the chapel, there are smaller rooms on the left side of the building that hold prayer rugs for your use.

Location

Zachry Room 300E

This room is open for any religion to use up to 5:30pm. It is located on the third floor of Zachry. It is all the way to the right if you are looking at the Electrical Measurements Lab.
